Location: Solace system
Date: 201.06.11

The BRS Prime Mover, a superfreighter on a mission to resupply an important outpost, has been ambushed. A sizeable merc fleet materialized out of hyperspace close to a gas giant, catching the fleet admiral off guard - however, he was able to contact a Blackrock-loyal privateer fleet orbiting the planet and rendezvous before the enemy fleet closed into engagement range.

The enemy is Black Fox, a notable mercenary leader, known to have close ties to Tri-Tachyon and an affinity for industrial sabotage.

There is just one fact that might tip the scales in Blackrock's favor: earlier, the BRS Shadowcat was brought in to test the interdimensional flow density near the big planets of the system, and it has been prepped for combat. As the captain of the BRS Shadowcat, you will be the centrepiece in the defense against the encroaching fleet. Retreat will merely allow the enemy to regroup on an intercept course: you need to crush them decisively here.
