The sound of fingers tapping against metal made a beat against the repeated sensor pings of the incoming high-energy signatures. Sitting down, the captain let out a long sigh.

"I thought we had eyes on this point not two hours ago. What the hell is TT doing out here?"

A young man stepped up, clutching a datapad to his chest.

"Tactical projections show-" 

The captain silenced his second-in command by subvocalizing a command to start the arming procedure, causing a brief klaxxon outburst followed by a slowly increasing hum that the bridge crew could feel in their skulls.

"I'm sure they're excited about the readings," the Captain said as he leaned in. "Let's give them a close-up for good measure."
